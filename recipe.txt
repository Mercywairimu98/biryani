1.Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes
2. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown
3.Add garlic,ginger and tomatoes.
4.Add chicken and cook till tender.
5.Add biriyani masala and salt
6.Boil rice separately until half cooked
7.Layer rice and chicken mixture.
8.Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
9.Serve hot.
